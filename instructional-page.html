Who is eligible for the Offcie 365 Onboarding Benefit?

New Customers that purchase at least 150 licenses for the new Office 365 tenantin one of the following plans for Office 356 on or after September 1st, 2014 are eligible to receie the FastTrack Onboarding benefit:

  • Office 365 Enterprise plans (E1,E3,E4)
  • Exchange Online Plan 1 and Exchange Online Plan 2
  • Office 365 Pro Plus, SharePoint Online standalong plans, Skype for Business standalone plans, OneDrive Standalone plans.
  • Office 365 Government plans (G1,G3,G4)
  • Paid Office 365 Education plans (A3,A4)
